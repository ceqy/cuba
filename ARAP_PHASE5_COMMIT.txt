- `get_account_balance()` - Calculate account balance
- `get_overdue_items()` - Filter overdue items for a partner

**CreditManagementService:**
- `perform_credit_check()` - **Full business logic implementation**
  - Retrieves customer credit limit
  - Calculates current exposure from open items
  - Determines check result (Pass/Warning/Fail)
  - Applies business rules:
    - FAIL: Balance exceeds credit limit
    - WARNING: Utilization > 90% or no limit set
    - PASS: Within acceptable range
  - Persists credit check record

**PaymentService:**
- `create_payment_proposal()` - Create payment proposal
- `approve_payment_proposal()` - Approve proposal for execution
- `execute_payment_run()` - Execute payment run
- `update_payment_run_status()` - Update run status
