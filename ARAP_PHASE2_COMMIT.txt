## Summary
Extended database schema with enterprise-grade tables for clearing, credit management,
payment processing, aging analysis, and banking features.

## New Database Migrations

### 0002_extended_tables.sql (8 Tables)
- **clearing_history**: Clearing document headers
- **clearing_items**: Line items for clearing operations
- **credit_limit_history**: Credit limit change tracking
- **credit_checks**: Credit check execution records
- **payment_proposals**: Payment run proposals
- **payment_proposal_items**: Proposal line items
- **aging_snapshots**: Historical aging analysis snapshots
- **dunning_history**: Customer dunning/collection records
- **account_statements**: Account statement generation

### 0003_bank_payments.sql (7 Tables)
- **bank_master**: Bank master data
- **partner_bank_accounts**: Partner banking details
- **payment_runs**: Payment execution runs
- **payment_run_items**: Individual payments in a run
- **advance_payments**: Prepayments/advances
- **advance_payment_applications**: Advance application tracking

### 0004_views_functions.sql (Views & Functions)
**Views:**
- `v_partner_overview`: Unified partner view (customer + supplier)
- `v_open_items_summary`: Aggregated open items
- `v_aging_analysis`: Real-time aging buckets
- `v_customer_credit_exposure`: Credit utilization tracking

**Functions:**
- `get_account_balance()`: Calculate partner balance
- `get_days_overdue()`: Calculate overdue days
- `calculate_payment_discount()`: Payment term discounts

**Triggers:**
- Auto-update open item clearing status
- Auto-update advance payment balances
